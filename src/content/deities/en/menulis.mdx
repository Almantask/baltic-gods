## Tidebound Light

Mėnulis keeps watch where rivers widen. His followers believe that moonlit water reveals hidden fords and warns of ambush. To travel without his notice is to invite a wandering light to mislead you until dawn.

## Nocturne Rituals

At full moon, boats are rubbed with ash so they disappear against the shine. Children are taught to count seven reflected stars before drinking from a night spring, a minor pact to keep dreams calm.
