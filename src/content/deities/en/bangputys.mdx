## Meeting of Winds

Bangpūtys is honored in the pause between gusts. Fishermen whisper into bone whistles, gauging which face of the wind listens. The northern face brings icy clarity; the southern face carries roaring surf and amber.

## Sailors' Ritual

Before departure, captains tie a knot in a strip of linen and promise to unbind it only when home. If the knot tightens itself mid-voyage, the crew knows Bangpūtys demands caution and a lower sail.
