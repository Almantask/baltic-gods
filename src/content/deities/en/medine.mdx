## Keeper of Quiet Paths

Medinė protects the forest interior. Woodcutters who rush find their blades dulled overnight. Those who bow before the first swing claim to hear the forest breathe back permission.

## Bark Prayers

Bark slips etched with names are tucked into crevices, sealed with resin. If the slip remains after a storm, the request is granted; if it falls, the seeker must choose another grove.
