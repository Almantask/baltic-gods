## Quiet Apex

Dievas is seldom invoked aloud. His worship is found in the alignment of tools, in bread split evenly, and in bells that ring once and then allow silence to reign. He is the sky that holds all other motions together.

## Ordered Night

On solstice eves, elders gather to align staffs with Polaris, marking how far the year has drifted. They note changes as messages from Dievas about balance between kin and land, adjusting planting cycles in response.
