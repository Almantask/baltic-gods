## Measured Steps

Laima speaks in the hush between steps on pine needles. Those who listen leave offerings of copper and flax at the water's lip, believing the ripples will stitch their intentions into the path ahead. Her songs are soft enough to tilt a hunt toward mercy.

## Threads and Outcomes

Weddings pause when Laima's name is murmured; a braid is tightened and cut to free a pair into shared luck. In dim attics, elders chart the direction of the cut thread against rafters, marking fates in charcoal that will outlast the season's storms.
