## Mounds that Listen

Žemyna is invoked by pressing both hands into the soil until the pulse of worms and roots is felt. Her altars are low and damp; offerings seep slowly and do not rush the ground. Farmers call her name before sharpening a plow so the iron knows its task is gentle.

## Harvests Held Close

At dusk, her followers lay bread on moss for the small creatures that serve the soil. They say she keeps record of every crumb returned. When fields fail, it is believed the loam has been starved of stories, so elders recite lineage beneath the open sky to feed it words again.
