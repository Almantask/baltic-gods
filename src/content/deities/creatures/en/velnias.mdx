## In the Hollow Ways

Velnias haunts the quiet routes where maps fade. He is not wholly cruel; he rewards courtesy and punishes arrogance. Locals toss crumbs and copper at forked paths, believing he will bend the fog around wolves and lead them astray.

## Bargains in Low Voices

Fishermen whisper a promise into the wind before casting nets, asking Velnias to tangle storms instead of sails. If the promise is broken, sandbars shift overnight. His presence is a reminder that respect is the only reliable compass in deep forest and deep night.
