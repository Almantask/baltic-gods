## Storm Lineage

Under a slate sky, Perkūnas strides in a belt of sparks. Oaks lean toward the flash, and granaries shake loose their dust to greet him. Along the Nemunas and Daugava, shepherds still hang iron keys to ring against cloud-thunder, asking the sky to spare their folds.

## Ritually Tracked Fire

Hammered bronze axes mark the points where lightning kissed the earth. Villages polish the scars with amber oil and chant of a god who keeps law with a single strike. Travelers whisper that every road between hill-forts is only safe when his rumble is heard twice.
