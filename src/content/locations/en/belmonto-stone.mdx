The Belmonto Stone is a massive mythological boulder located in the scenic Pavilniai Regional Park near Vilnius. Local folklore describes it as a silent witness to ancient oaths and a place where offerings were left to the earth mother, Žemyna.

For the ancient Lithuanians, such stones were not mere objects, but sentient parts of the landscape. The Belmonto Stone continues to be a site of geological wonder and cultural continuity, a solid anchor in the shifting mists of the Vilnia valley.
