Birutė's Hill is a towering landmark on the Palanga coast, forever associated with the sacred fire and the priestess who guarded it. Birutė, a historical and mythologized figure, is said to have chosen the path of a Vaidilutė before her marriage to Duke Kęstutis.

Today, the hill stands as a monument to the endurance of Baltic traditions. Visitors often feel the sharp sea wind and the warmth of the setting sun, much like the priestesses did while maintaining the eternal flame that connected the community to Gabija.
