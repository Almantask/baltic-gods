Šatrija Hill is the legendary capital of Samogitian magic. It is said that every Midsummer night, witches from across the Baltic lands fly here on brooms or invisible steeds to hold their secret sabbaths and celebrate the peak of the sun's power.

Archaeologically, the hill is a massive hillfort, but in the popular consciousness, it is a site of immense feminine power and occult knowledge. Even today, people gather on its flat summit to watch the sunset, feeling the heavy ancient aura that makes it the most famous mythological hill in the region.
