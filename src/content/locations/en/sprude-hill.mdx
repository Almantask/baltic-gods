Sprūdė Hillfort is a massive defensive complex that dominates the Samogitian landscape. Linked by legend to the dynastic struggles of King Mindaugas's family, the hill was a bastion of vigilance, guarding the spiritual heart of the region against both internal and external threats.

The hill's scale reflects the organizational power of the Samogitian nobility. It was a place where the community gathered to reaffirm their connection to the high gods, ensuring that their defense was sanctified by the ancient laws of order and sovereignty.
