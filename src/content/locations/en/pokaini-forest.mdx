Pokaiņi Forest is a landscape of mystery, where thousands of moss-covered stone piles are scattered among the trees in intentional, albeit unknown, patterns. Many consider it an anomalous energy zone, an ancient healing sanctuary where the earth speaks through the cold stone.

Contemporary followers of Baltic traditions use the forest as a site for meditation and energy work, believing that the arrangement of the stones was designed by ancient seers to channel cosmic forces for the benefit of the living.
