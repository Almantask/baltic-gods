The Turaida Sacred Spring flows from the red sandstone of the Gauja valley, its waters blessed by Laima. It is a place of beginning and hope, where the weaver of fate is most accessible to human prayers.

Midwives bring the first linens of newborns to be rinsed in these cold, clear waters. It is believed that the patterns the water makes on the cloth are signs of Laima's favor, deciding whether the child's thread of life will be smooth or knotted.
