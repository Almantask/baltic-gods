The Ķemeri marshes and sulfur springs are a testament to the grounding, purifying power of the earth. In ancient times, the sulfurous 'breath' of the springs was seen as a manifestation of the underworld spirits, offering purification and healing to those who approached with the correct rites.

The vast, boggy landscape was a place of isolation and spiritual trial. It remains a site that reminds us of the raw, untamed forces of nature—the realm of Velnias and the more ancient, impersonal spirits of the mist and the mud.
