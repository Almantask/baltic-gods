The Lopaičiai complex is a place where the modern and ancient spiritualities of Samogitia converge. It is not just a single site, but a landscape of sacred features: an altar stone, a healing spring, and what many believe to be an ancient astronomical observatory.

Vistors often leave small stones or ribbons at the 'priesthood stone', seeking the wisdom of the ancient seers. The water from the spring is said to stay fresh for months, carrying the blessing of Žemyna to those who drink it with respect.
