Near the Šventoji river stands the sanctuary of the colossal oaks, the primary earthly seat of Perkūnas on the coast. The air here often smells of ozone and ancient bark, even on the clearest of days.

Priests once maintained an eternal fire at the heart of this grove, fueled by the wood of oaks naturally struck by lightning. Today, people still leave iron nails and scrap metal at the roots, hoping the sky lord will take the offering and spare their homes from his bolts.
