The Samogitian Sanctuary (*Žemaičių alka*) in Šventoji is more than a reconstruction. It is a portal to the ancient Baltic understanding of the cosmos, where the positions of the sun, moon, and stars governed the rhythms of tribal life and sacred time.

This modern shrine is based on archaeological finds of ancient observatories. It serves as a reminder of the sophisticated celestial knowledge possessed by the Baltic priests (*žyniai*), who translated the movements of the universe into ritual action for the community.
