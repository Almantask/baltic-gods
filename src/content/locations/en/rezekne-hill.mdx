Rēzekne Castle Hill stands as the historic high ground of Latgale. Long before the stone fortresses of the crusaders, this hill was a major fortified settlement where the Latgalian people gathered for council and fire-ceremonies.

The hill represents the resilience of Latgalian identity. It was a site where the community observed the celestial cycles, ensuring that their actions were in harmony with the cosmos and the watchful eye of Perkūnas, the guardian of justice and order.
