The Daugavas Loki (Daugava Bends) are nine spectacular curves where the river winds through the ancient valley of Latgale. This area is often called the 'Mythological Spine' of Latvia, as the river has guided the migration and spiritual lives of Baltic tribes for millennia.

The bends are seen as loops of fate woven by the goddess Laima, with each curve representing a different challenge or blessing in the life of the nation. The landscape remains one of the most untouched and spiritually resonant river valleys in Northern Europe.
