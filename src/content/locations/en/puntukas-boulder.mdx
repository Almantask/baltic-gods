Puntukas is a titan of stone resting in the Anykščiai forest. According to the most famous legend, the devil himself was carrying this rock to crush the local church, but the sudden crowing of a rooster at dawn forced him to drop it where it stands today.

Beyond the legends, Puntukas has served as a landmark and an altar for centuries. Its immense size and the moss that covers its grey surface give it the appearance of a sleeping creature, a silent observer of the pine forest's many secrets and the god of the shadows, Velnias.
