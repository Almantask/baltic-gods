The Verkiai Grove in Vilnius was once one of the most significant sites for the cult of Perkūnas. According to legend, it was here that the high priest Lizdeika was found as a child in an eagle's nest, later becoming the primary advisor to Grand Duke Gediminas.

The grove was the site of the sacred eternal fire, maintained by priestesses known as Vaidilutės. Even today, the ancient oaks of Verkiai are said to hold the resonance of the rituals that once took place under their massive branches, connecting the modern city to its pagan heart.
