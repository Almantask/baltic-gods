Witches' Hill in Juodkrantė is a place where the fog of the Curonian Spit meets the vivid imagination of folk artists. The sand dune has long been a place of Midsummer gatherings, where the line between the human and spirit worlds is said to grow thin.

The wooden sculptures that now line the paths depict a chaotic and beautiful pantheon: from the trickster Velnias and the towering giantess Neringa to the various witches and demons of the Baltic night. It remains a site of laughter, storytelling, and a deep, coastal mystery.
