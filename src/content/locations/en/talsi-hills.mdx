Talsi is often called the 'Town on Nine Hills', with its ancient castle mound serving as the spiritual anchor for the Curonian people. For centuries, this complex was a hub of tribal administration and ritual, overlooking the lakes and forests of Kurzeme.

The hills are seen as a natural constellation, representing the interconnectedness of fate and the earth. Talsi remains a symbol of Curonian persistence, where the ancient rhythms of life are still felt in the winds that sweep across the ridges of the heart of Kurzeme.
