The Anykščiai Forest, or Anykščių šilelis, is a place where the pine needles whisper with the voices of the past. It is said that Medeina, the forest goddess, monitors every footstep from the shadows of the tall spruces.

Hunters entering these woods must first leave a small piece of bread or a tuft of wool at the base of the oldest pines. If the spectral hare crosses their path, it is a sign from the goddess that the hunt is forbidden in that sector for the day.
