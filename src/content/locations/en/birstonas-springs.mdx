The Birštonas springs, located in the scenic loops of the Nemunas river, have been a site of healing and spiritual restoration for millennia. The mineral-rich waters were seen by the ancient Lithuanians as the medicinal 'milk' of the earth goddess, Žemyna.

This ancient healing sanctuary offered a space for purification and the balancing of the spirit. The gentle mists that rise from the river loops create a natural buffer from the outside world, making Birštonas a timeless refuge for those seeking to reconnect with the nurturing forces of the Baltic earth.
