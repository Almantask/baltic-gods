Zilaiskalns, the 'Blue Hill', is the spiritual crown of Vidzeme. Often draped in a distinctive blue mist, it has been a center for divination and communal gatherings for as long as memory serves. It was traditionally a site where the 'Vaideločiai' (priests) interpreted the signs of the gods.

The hill is also famous for its healing properties, with countless stories of people seeking its summit to be cured of ailments or to understand their fate. In modern times, it remains a focal point for those seeking to reconnect with the ancient Baltic worldview.
