The Tytuvėnai sacred spring in Samogitia has been a site for healing and spiritual restoration for as long as memory remains. Since pre-Christian times, the spring's waters were believed to be blessed by the goddess of fate, Laima.

The spring represents the pure, life-giving power of the Samogitian land. Its clear waters, as gifts of the earth mother, were believed by the ancestors to wash away both physical pain and spiritual uncertainty, washing clean the path toward the future.
