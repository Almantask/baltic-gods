The lake of Limbaži, in the Vidzeme heartlands, is far more than a source of fresh water; it is viewed by the local people as a sentient entity with its own spirits and guardian deities. Since time immemorial, the lake's depths were seen as a repository for sacred offerings and a source of wisdom.

Limbažu Ezers remains a site where the community pays respect to the fluid, life-giving power of the landscape, where the mists of the early morning are thought to be the breath of the lake-spirits rising to meet the sky-gods.
