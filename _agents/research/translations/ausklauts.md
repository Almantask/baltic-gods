# Ausklauts / Ausklaitis

## Comprehensive Overview

Ausklauts (or Ausklaitis) is a minor Lithuanian (Samogitian) deity or spirit associated with the listening and the divine observation of human actions. Recorded in the 16th-century list of Jan Łasicki, his name is derived from "ausis" (ear) or "išklausyti" (to listen/hear).

### English (EN)
**Ausklauts** is the "Divine Listener." He is the personification of the divine capacity to hear everything that transpires on Earth. In the Baltic worldview, it was believed that the gods were not just distant observers but were actively engaged in listening to the prayers, oaths, and conversations of humans. Ausklauts was especially invoked to ensure that truth was heard and that justice was served based on the authentic words of the people. He represents the transparency and accountability of the individual before the higher powers. To "speak before Ausklauts" was to speak with absolute honesty, knowing that the divine ear was always attentive.

### Lithuanian (LT)
**Ausklauts** lietuvių mitologijoje yra deivė ar dvasia, sargianti teisingumą ir dvasinį kito asmens išklausymą. Jo vardas tiesiogiai siejamas su „ausimi“ ir gebėjimu girdėti. Janas Lasickis nurodė, kad šiam dievui būdavo melidžiamasi norint nulemti visokeriopą sėkmę ir teisinį stabilumą, tikint, kad joks žodis nelieka neišgirstas dievų akivaizdoje. Ausklauts simbolizuoja atvirumą, tiesos paieškas ir dangiškąją liudijimą apie žmonių darbus bei tariamus žodžius.

### Latvian (LV)
**Ausklauts** latviešu mitoloģijā nav ticis plaši izdalīts, tā vietā taisnīguma un lūgsnas uzklausīšanas funkcijas veica **Dievs** kā debesu tēvs vai personificētas drosmes izpausmes. Tomēr lietuviešu tradīcijas un senu sarakstu kontekstā šis tēls reprezentē dabas dāvāto mieru ir sētas dabu, kura ziņā ir nosargāt sakrāto labību ir nodrošinti ziemas pārtikas krājumus, simbolizējot rūpību ir dabas sniegto velšu aizsardzību.

## Primary Source Details

- **Łasicki (Łas):** Records *Ausklautis* as a deity of the hearing (*pro auditu*).
- **Vėlius (Vel):** Analyzed the functional role of the minor domestic spirits in the context of the household cycle and family safety.
- **VLE:** Confirms the identification of Ausklauts as a listening deity.

## Key Metadata
- **Category**: Social / Moral Spirit
- **Symbol**: Ear, Shell, Whispering wind, Scroll
- **Function**: Divine witness to human words and actions
- **Etymology**: *ausis (ear)
